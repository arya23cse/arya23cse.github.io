Symptoms of Weak Mental Health

Depression

  • Persistent sadness or feelings of emptiness
  • Lack of interest in activities once enjoyed
  • Changes in appetite or weight
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Loss of energy or fatigue

Anxiety

  • Excessive worrying or fear
  • Feeling restless or on edge
  • Difficulty controlling worrying thoughts
  • Panic attacks
  • Trouble sleeping or insomnia

Stress

  • Feeling overwhelmed or constantly under pressure
  • Changes in appetite or sleep patterns
  • Irritability or anger
  • Physical symptoms like headaches or muscle tension
  • Difficulty relaxing or constant racing thoughts

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, please seek professional help.
